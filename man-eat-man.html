Devlog
Man Eat Man is a medieval turn-based strategy RPG set in a fantasy world inhabited by zoomorphic humans and all manner of terrible monsters. Humanity is reduced to a derisive parody of its lost favor as the Universe itself refutes our dominion of Earth.
Seize control of a band of warriors vying for territory and survival in a hostile land. Fend off invasions from rival factions, eliminate banditry and corruption from your lands, and repel the encroaching evil that seeks to extinguish our very humanity.
Witness medieval military history like never before as you conquer your foes across exciting strategy gameplay scenarios. Unleash your inner beast and carve your name out in destiny's heart!
Man Eat Man brings a never-before-seen dark fantasy world roaring to life in authentic medieval fashion!
      Manage your holdings and strategize:
  • Organize labor to gather and refine resources.
  • Shelter and feed your subjects to ensure their health.
  • Command armies and lay siege to your opponents' strongholds!
      • Marshal your troops in tactical combat:
  • Lead your soldiers into battle; positioning is crucial.
  • Utilize unique weapon classes to optimize your offensive potential.
